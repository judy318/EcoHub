Welcome to EcoHub, a website devoted to helping the environment, and forming a community!

What is EcoHub?

EcoHub was created by High School students in order to help inspire people to make a difference and help the environment. Eco Hub has tons of information on topics such as endangered species to helping the environment. We also have a forum for you to use to discuss the new information you have learned, as well as other ways you have found to help the planet!
